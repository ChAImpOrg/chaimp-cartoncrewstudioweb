Why CartonCrewStudio

Your world, finally
modelled the way it works.

Most CRMs make you bend a one-size-fits-all “constituent” record around an artist, or file a piece under “notes.” CartonCrewStudio is CartonCrew tuned for the arts: the same lean, no-bloat CRM we run ChAImp on — but it knows your world. It gives you the things you actually work with, and an AI that quietly remembers the relationships that keep a gallery alive.

First-class records

Built around what a gallery really runs on.

Five record types, designed for the arts from the ground up — connected, searchable, and ready the day you open the doors.

🎨

Artists

Full profiles, representation history, mediums, and the story behind every relationship — not a flattened contact row.

🖼️

Works

Catalogue each piece with provenance, medium, dimensions, price, and status — sold, pledged, on loan, or available.

🏛️

Exhibitions

Plan shows end to end: which artists, which works, which patrons to invite, and what happened after the opening.

🤝

Patrons

Collectors, members, and donors with the context that matters — what they bought, pledged, and which artists they follow.

✨

Programs

Classes, residencies, and community workshops — the mission-driven work, tracked alongside everything else.

AI relationship memory

conversations stay human.

Underneath the records, the same AI relationship memory that powers CartonCrew keeps track of which collector follows which artist, what a patron bought or pledged, and who’s due for a show. It surfaces the next right move — so you spend your time on people, not spreadsheets.
